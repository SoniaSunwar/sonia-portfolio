My Story

Hi, I’m Sonia, and I’m a UX Designer with a background as Structural CAD Technician.

During my school, I have always enjoyed computer science, particularly the practical side of it because it was more fun and exciting. Now, I would like to connect to my younger self by working in the tech industry. After researching different tech roles I was drawn more towards UX Design and Front End Development.

I also found that there are some similarities with what I am already doing, for example understanding the client's needs, collaborating with different faculty teams working towards the same goal and delivering quality design.

I have completed my UX design course and have been enjoying my specialisation course in Front End Development. I am looking forward to working on various projects where I can use my knowledge and create meaningful designs.

Apart from work and study, I like to travel to different countries, play badminton with friends and cooking.
