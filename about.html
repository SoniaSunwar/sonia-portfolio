My Story

I'm Sonia, a UX Designer with a background as a Structural Computer-Aided Design (CAD) Technician.

Following the completion of my Master's degree in Civil Engineering, I embarked on a career in the construction and design industry. A few years into my career, I rekindled my interest in technology, which led me to a strong attraction to UX design because of its human-centered design approach. To gain a comprehensive understanding of UX design, I completed a 10-month course encompassing UX design and Front-End Development through CareerFoundry.

I eagerly anticipate the opportunity to contribute my knowledge and skills to various projects, where I can create meaningful designs that make a difference.
